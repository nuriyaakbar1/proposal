About Us

Founded in 2012, ProSport Hub is a leading South African retailer of high-quality sports equipment, footwear, and official team jerseys. From grassroots athletes to professionals, we are passionate about promoting active lifestyles and empowering individuals to achieve their personal best. Our mission is to equip individuals with the tools they need to reach peak performance in sports, and our vision is to become Africa’s most trusted supplier of sports gear. Over the years, ProSport Hub has built a reputation for delivering reliable products, expert service, and authentic sporting apparel. We proudly serve athletes, schools, sports clubs, and sports enthusiasts nationwide. Whether you’re looking for performance-driven footwear, top-quality equipment, or official jerseys, ProSport Hub is here to support your sporting journey!
